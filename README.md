# About

AI/ML Engineer with a B.E. in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science (CGPA 8.98/10) and hands-on industry experience at Cognizant designing, fine-tuning, and deploying production-grade machine learning and Generative AI systems. My work spans the complete lifecycle of AI product development — from dataset curation and model experimentation to REST API development, evaluation pipelines, deployment automation, and scalable inference infrastructure.

I specialize in **Large Language Models, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), Agentic AI workflows, and production ML engineering**. I have built stateful AI assistants, fine-tuned open-weight transformer models (Llama 3, Mistral, Phi) using PEFT/LoRA, engineered automated evaluation frameworks, and developed backend systems capable of serving enterprise AI workloads efficiently at scale.

I approach every problem with a **software engineering and product mindset**, emphasizing maintainability, observability, security, performance optimization, and measurable business impact. I operate comfortably across the full ML stack — from raw tokenization and model quantization through to production-grade FastAPI services in Dockerized Linux environments.
